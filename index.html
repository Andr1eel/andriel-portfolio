SLAM-Style Autonomous Navigation System

Built a C++ simulation of a full SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) pipeline. A robot with a 40-pixel simulated LiDAR performs clockwise boundary mapping, interior sweeping with obstacle avoidance, then BFS pathfinding to reach a goal cell - all in one autonomous sequence. Averaged 85-90% environment mapping accuracy across 5 runs.

Rubber Band-Powered Self-Parking Car

Designed and laser-cut an acrylic vehicle - no electronics - that knocks over Jenga blocks and self-parks with 3 wheels within the lines. Used an independent dual-axle system with 7" side rods for block contact and differential torque for directional control. My concept was selected from 7 team designs via a weighted evaluation matrix, scoring highest at 4.2/5. Fully modelled in SolidWorks and fabricated from a single 8"x10" acrylic sheet.

CycloHome - Bike-Towed Collapsible Shelter

Designed a 2m x 1.5m bicycle-towed trailer that deploys into a pyramid shelter through a telescopic mast and 8-arm linkage in under 7 minutes with less than 100N of setup force. The concept focused on inclusive use, with solar-powered LEDs, a 15kg storage drawer, a corrosion-resistant aluminum frame, and a tool-free hitch.
